Australian Malayalee Islamic Association (AMIA), Victoria, Australia, a not for profit organization founded in 2009 in Melbourne is an initiative by the likeminded Muslim brothers from Kerala (state in India) living in Victoria with the primary objective of helping and being supportive each other in the initial phase of one’s migration and thereafter also to foster a friendly, mutually supportive and learning environment for the entire family by holding on to Islamic teachings, values and principles and to involve in charity works within the community

AMIA conducts the following ongoing activities

  • Monthly get-together of all its members (First Saturday of every month)
  • Involve in social and multicultural events and Local community programs
  • Arrange scholastic information sharing session on religion, social and cultural entities
  • As part of the religious harmony, involve with all sections of the society in religious functions, get-togethers especially during the holy month of Ramadan (month of fasting).
